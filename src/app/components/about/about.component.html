Who We Are

VSS Enterprises is a registered electrical & electronic contracting firm with a strong track record of delivering turnkey projects for government departments, public sector units, industrial facilities and private commercial clients. Our team of licensed electricians and qualified engineers brings technical precision and deep field experience to every project — from initial design and load calculation through to final testing, commissioning and handover.

We are fully compliant with Indian electrical standards (IS/IEC) and the Indian Electricity Act, and we carry out every assignment with the highest commitment to safety, quality workmanship and on-time delivery. Whether it is a single-phase house wiring job or a large-scale industrial power distribution system, VSS Enterprises brings the same level of expertise and professionalism.
